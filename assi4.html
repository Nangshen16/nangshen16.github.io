Roman drinking fountains

A nasone (plural nasoni), also called a fontanella (plural fontanelle, lit. "little fountains"), is a type of drinking fountain found in Rome, Italy. ... There are approximately 2,500–2,800 nasoni in Rome, supplying people with free drinking water..
